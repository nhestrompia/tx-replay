## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are active Hyperliquid traders reviewing completed positions. They use this product to inspect what happened during a trade, validate entries/exits, and understand how market context affected PnL.

### Brand Personality
Focused, analytical, and trustworthy. The interface should feel like a precision instrument: calm under pressure, dense with signal, and free from decorative noise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark trading-console UI with crisp typography, restrained accents, and strong contrast for fast scanning. The design should feel opinionated and technical without becoming visually aggressive.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize time-to-insight: key numbers and controls should be visible without hunting.
2. Use hierarchy through contrast and spacing, not repetitive boxed containers.
3. Keep high-frequency actions obvious and low-friction (playback, filtering, replay jump).
4. Treat status and PnL as semantic signals with consistent color and badge behavior.
5. Favor compact, information-rich layouts that remain readable on smaller screens.
